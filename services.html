What We Offer

Built on trust, transparency, and reliability - your dependable link between South Africa and Zimbabwe.

Cross-Border Shopping & Procurement

Tell us what you need, and we'll handle the rest. We purchase goods from reputable suppliers in South Africa on your behalf. From groceries and electronics to building materials and personal items, we ensure you get quality products at competitive prices.

Secure Warehousing

Need a safe place to store your goods before they are shipped? We provide secure, temporary warehousing for small to medium-sized packages. This service offers flexibility, allowing you to consolidate multiple purchases before sending them off to Zimbabwe.

Reliable Transportation

We partner with trusted cross-border bus services like Tamuka, Swiss Express, and others to transport your goods. These partnerships ensure your items are handled professionally and delivered on time, every time.

Transparent Pricing

Our business model is simple and honest. We charge a set percentage on the total value of your order for our procurement and logistics services. There are no hidden fees, ensuring you know exactly what you're paying for.
